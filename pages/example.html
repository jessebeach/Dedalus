The Storm

Dark clouds build on the horizon.

Our hero can sense something is about to happen. She can feel the energy in the air. Suddenly a great clap of thunder shook the house.

As the sound of thunder echoed and withdrew in the distance, our hero noticed a small box on the table that had not been there a moment ago. The box is covered in ornate carvings and symbols.
